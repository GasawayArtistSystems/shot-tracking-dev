Timeline

Timelines are essential to keeping projects on track. This will show how they are initially setup, edited, and used to help any production.

Timelines are divided between Pre-Production, Assets, and Production.

Period

Clicking on the Day Week Month Year will change the view of the timeline.

Current Day

The Current day is shown by the red dashed line:

Editing Start/End Dates

To edit the start or end date of a section, drag the left or right bar to extend or shrink the length of time.

Notes about Timeline

Changing the start or end date on the timeline affects assets, scenes, and shots ONLY before those assets, scenes, and shots are started. Once they are created, the timeline won't affect the start and end dates on the individual assets, scenes, and shots. Those can be adjusted in the assets, scenes, or shots themselves.
