Grading

This page will show how grading works in the app. It will show how to change the status of an assignment and of shots or assets in a film. Grading for films is exactly like approving shots.

📌REMEMBER: The DASHBOARD is where all statuses are changed. Only UPMs and Coodinators may have access to other parts of the software.

STUDENTS - Changing Status on Assignments

NOTE!: Maya has a built in Sumitting process. Go to the Maya page to see how that works.

Once an assignment has been submitted to be graded or a shot has been submitted to be approved, the next step is to change the status ONLY if you want to change the grade.

  1. The STATUS of your assignment is now switched to Submitted
    1. Your instructor is informed that your assignment is ready to be graded.
  2. Instructor grades assignment and may include a markup.
    1. When it's graded, the status of your assignment may change:
      1. If graded under a 5, the status will change to Retake
      2. If graded a 5, the status will change to Graded. The assignment is considered completed!
    2. There may be a review to watch what you can change/delete/add to get a better grade.
    3. If it's less than a 5, you have the option of making changes to get a better grade.

Grades

The grades are simplified to show you where you are in the assignment. MOST assignments, unless otherwise noted, may be turned in again for a possible higher score.

The following is the scale used for most assignments:

  • F - 0 - Nothing submitted
  • D - 1 - Not much effort put forth
  • C - 2 - Average student work
  • B - 3 - Good student work
  • A - 4 - Very good student work
  • A+ - 5 - Excellent good student work
📌REMEMBER: The DASHBOARD is where all statuses live. This will always show you what is due and what your current grades for assignments are. there may also be Reviews to watch a video, explaining what is needed to improve.

INSTRUCTORS - Changing Status on Assignments

The best way to grade assignments is through the Markup Tool. This allows the instructor to go through each animation frame by frame and add drawings ontop of each frame. They can then assign a letter grade that automatically gets updated in the application.

Assignments can be graded through the main app as well. Simply go to the class and then assignment. This individual assignment page will show all the individual assignments for every student in the class. It will also show each status of each step.

If the assignment is submitted, the FB (feedback) status will change to Grading.

This means it's ready for you to grade.

You can change the grade here without using a markup. If the grade is less than a 5, it will automatically change the status to Retake. It is then up to the student if they want to do the retake or can change the grade to Graded.

COORDINATORS - Changing Status on Shots/Assets

Approvals are handled through the Markup Tool.

This allows the director, upm, coordinator, and artists to go through each animation frame by frame and add drawings ontop of each frame. They can then approve or call a retake on the shot or asset.
